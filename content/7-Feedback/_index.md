> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) program.

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is extremely friendly, open, and dynamic. The workspace is comfortable and professional, fostering high productivity.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor provides dedicated, detailed guidance while encouraging interns to take initiative in problem-solving. The admin team is highly supportive and responsive with administrative tasks.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
Assigned tasks are closely aligned with the IT major, consolidating university knowledge while expanding into practical cloud computing applications.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
Excellent learning opportunities through hands-on experience with modern AWS services, along with developing teamwork, project management, and professional communication skills.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
A respectful, positive, and collaborative culture. All team members are always ready to support each other to achieve shared goals.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
Clear internship support policies, flexible working hours, and valuable opportunities to attend high-quality internal training sessions.
